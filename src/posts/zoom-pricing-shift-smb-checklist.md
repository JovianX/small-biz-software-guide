Vendors rarely rename products for fun. When pricing shifts, the fastest risk for small businesses is **auto-renewal drift**: you keep the same habits, but the invoice changes.

## What to verify this week

- **Renewal cadence** and whether annual vs monthly pricing moved.
- **Add-ons** attached to a few power users that became “team defaults.”
- **Meeting types** you actually use (webinars, phone bridges, cloud recording) vs what your plan includes.

We will update this note with specifics as the change set stabilizes.

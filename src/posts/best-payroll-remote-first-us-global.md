Payroll is where “we will handle taxes” meets **your actual liability** if filings drift. For small teams, the buying decision is less about brand and more about **operational tolerance**: how much variance you have in pay types, states, and contractor mix.

## Hidden issues we look for

- **Implementation timelines** when you add new states mid-year.
- **Off-cycle runs** and corrections—how painful, how audited.
- **Benefits bundling** that quietly increases per-seat totals.

## Verdict (starter)

Optimize for accuracy and support responsiveness first; shiny employee self-service second.

This is a deliberately small roundup: not “50 tools,” but **a few lanes** that match how solo founders and 5–10 person teams actually sell.

## How we compare (without pretending it is lab-perfect)

- **Day-one setup**: imports, duplicates, and pipeline defaults.
- **Email + compliance footguns**: cold outreach habits vs what providers tolerate.
- **Reporting honesty**: forecasting features that look powerful but need clean data hygiene.

## What we are actively testing next

Sequences, call logging, and mobile workflows for founders who sell from the road.

## Verdict (starter)

Pick the CRM you can keep clean—not the one with the flashiest AI slide deck.

Founders often choose accounting software the way they choose a bank: **who integrates cleanly today**. The hidden issues show up when revenue gets lumpier: annual prepay, credits, partial refunds, and cross-border invoices.

## Hidden issues we look for

- **Bank reconciliation edge cases** when processors split fees weirdly.
- **Reporting packages** that require add-ons once boards ask sharper questions.
- **Advisor ecosystem**: who your firm prefers, and why that matters at tax time.

## Verdict (starter)

If your ops team is finance-light, bias toward whichever stack your accountant will defend under pressure—not whichever UI you prefer on day one.

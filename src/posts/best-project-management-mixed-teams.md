Project management fails small teams when the tool demands **process maturity** you do not have yet. The best fit is usually the one your least technical stakeholder can use without a training program.

## Hidden issues we look for

- **Default notification settings** (the real killer of adoption).
- **Guest/project permission models** for agencies and freelancers.
- **Reporting** that either helps owners prioritize, or becomes theater.

## Verdict (starter)

Pick boring workflows with loud defaults over clever workflows with quiet defaults.

QuickBooks Online is the default SMB ledger for a reason: banks connect, accountants recognize it, payroll exists. The hidden story is how **SKU tiers** and **feature gates** show up once you add people and dimensions.

## Hidden issues we look for

- **User seats and role friction** when owners want separation of duties without enterprise pricing.
- **Class/location reporting** that looks fine until inventory-ish workflows creep in.
- **Apps ecosystem costs**: the base product is rarely the final monthly bill.

## Verdict (starter)

A safe choice when your accountant is already in the ecosystem. Budget time to tame categories early, before historical cleanup becomes expensive.

If you already pay for Microsoft 365, Teams can feel “free.” If you live in Google Workspace, Slack can feel inevitable. The real comparison is **how your team communicates under stress**—incidents, launches, support escalations.

## Hidden issues we look for

- **Search quality** across messages and files once history grows.
- **Guest access models** when clients and agencies need limited visibility.
- **Mobile realities** for founders who approve things between meetings.

## Verdict (starter)

Teams wins when your world is already files-in-SharePoint and meetings-in-Outlook. Slack wins when channels are your system of record and integrations matter more than bundled meetings.
